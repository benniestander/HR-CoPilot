## üíº SWOT Analysis: Business Section

### üü¢ STRENGTHS
- **Self-Service Efficiency**: Business owners can generate documents in minutes without a legal retainer.
- **Legislative Currency**: Dynamic updates (e.g., Van Wyk judgment) keep small businesses compliant in real-time.
- **Low Barrier to Entry**: PAYG (Pay-As-You-Go) model is perfect for SME budgets.

### üü° WEAKNESSES
- **Integration Friction**: Business owners might find it hard to map AI fields to their actual HR software.
- **Complex Situations**: AI struggles with "gray area" disciplinary cases without human mediation.
- **Retention**: Once a business has its 20 core policies, their "need" for the tool drops until a legal change occurs.

### üîµ OPPORTUNITIES
- **Employee Portal**: Letting employees log in to view their specific policies/contracts.
- **BCEA Monitoring**: Proactive alerts when an employee's hours or leave balances hit legal limits.
- **Payroll Bridge**: Exporting policy data directly to South African payroll providers (SimplePay, Sage).

### üî¥ THREATS
- **Competitor Under-Pricing**: Low-cost template providers claiming "AI-powered."
- **Institutional Distrust**: Larger SMEs preferring a human lawyer over a "Copilot."
- **Regulatory Change Speed**: Failing to update the "Brain" fast enough after a major legislative shift.
